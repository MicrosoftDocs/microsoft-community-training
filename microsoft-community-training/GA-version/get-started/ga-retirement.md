# Community Training Retirement Notice 


Community Training (GA) has been permanently retired and is no longer accessible.
To continue training programs, customers must migrate to a Microsoft-recognized
Community Training partner solutions mentioned below:

1. [CT Nova Technologies](https://www.ctnova.io/)  offers a product called [CT Nova](https://www.ctnova.io/solutions), an AI-enhanced learning platform for Fortune 500 companies and nonprofit organizations.
[Reach out to CT Nova Technologies here to learn more about CT Nova.](https://www.ctnova.io/contact)

2. [Philanthrosoft](https://philanthrosoft.org/) offers a product called [ScaleEd](https://philanthrosoft.org/page3.html), a cloud-based learning platform designed for businesses, educational institutions, nonprofits, and government organizations.
[Reach out to Philanthrosoft here to learn more about ScaleEd.](https://philanthrosoft.org/page1.html)
